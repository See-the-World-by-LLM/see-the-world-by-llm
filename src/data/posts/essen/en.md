## Fun Facts About Essen

* **Green City**: Despite its industrial past, Essen is a green oasis with over 60 parks and gardens, earning it the title of "European Green City" in 2017.
* **Beer Lovers' Paradise**: Essen is home to the largest brewery in Germany, producing over 1.2 million hectoliters of beer annually. Cheers to that!
* **Museum Mile**: With over 50 museums, Essen boasts one of the highest concentrations of cultural institutions in Germany. Get ready to geek out!

## Local Food You Must Try

* **Krombacher Beer**: As the largest brewery in Germany, Krombacher is a must-try. Take a tour or simply enjoy a cold one at a local beer garden.
* **Ruhrpott Currywurst**: This Essen twist on the classic German snack features a spicy ketchup-based sauce and is typically served with fries or a roll.
* **Printen**: These traditional spiced cookies are a staple in the region. Try them with a cup of coffee for the ultimate local experience.
