## Fun Facts About Al Masna'ah

Here are three fun facts to get you started:

* Al Masna'ah is home to the **oldest fort in Oman**, the Al Masna'ah Fort, which dates back to the 17th century!
* The city is nestled in the **Al Hajar Mountains**, offering breathtaking views and a cooler climate than other parts of Oman.
* Al Masna'ah is known for its **traditional pottery**, with many local artisans still using ancient techniques to create stunning pieces.

## Local Food You Must Try

Omani cuisine is a fusion of Arabic, Indian, and African flavors, and Al Masna'ah is no exception! Be sure to try:

* **Shuwa**: a traditional Omani dish made with meat, spices, and rice, cooked in an underground oven.
* **Luqaimat**: sweet dumplings flavored with cardamom and saffron, typically served as a dessert or snack.
* **Kahwa**: a strong, cardamom-infused coffee that's an integral part of Omani hospitality.
